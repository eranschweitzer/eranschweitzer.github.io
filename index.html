Eran Schweitzer

Electrical Engineer

About

I am currently project engineer in the Energy Technologies department of STEAG Energy Services. I recently completed my PhD at the SINE lab at Arizona State University, under Professor Anna Scaglione. My professional interests fall broadly under the umbrella of power system modeling, analysis, and optimization. I have worked on several linearizations to the power flow problem, both global and local solver solutions to the optimal power flow, and several developments of unit commitment models.
